Abstract Factory

intent:
Abstract Factory is a creational design patern that lets you produce families of related objects
without specifying their concrete classes.


Problem:
Imagine that you are creating a furniture shop simulator, your code consis of classes that represent:
1: A family of related products, say: Chair + Sofa + CoffeTable
2: Sevral variats of this familiy for example , products Chair +Sofa + CoffeTable are avilable in these variants : Modern, Victorian, ArtDeco
